一様分布の順序統計量$U_{(k-1)}$, $U_{(k)}$の同時確率密度分布(導出はch5/q1.mdを参照)
$$
\begin{align*}
f_{X_{(k-1)}, X_{(k)}}(X_{(k-1)}=x, X_{(k)}=y) &= \frac{n!}{(k-2)!(n-k)!}F(x)^{k-2}f(x)f(y) (1-F(y))^{n-k}\\
&= \frac{n!}{(k-2)!(n-k)!}x^{k-2}(1-y)^{n-k}
\end{align*}
$$  

$w = y-x$, $z = x$とすると
$$
\begin{align*}
f(w, t) = \frac{n!}{(k-2)!(n-k)!}z^{k-2}(1-w-z)^{n-k}
\end{align*}

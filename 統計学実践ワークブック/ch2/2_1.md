## [1]

$$
\begin{align*}
&\int_0^1\int_0^1c(x+y)dxdy = 1\\
&c\int_0^1\left[xy + \frac{1}{2}y^2\right]_0^1dx = 1\\
&c\int_0^1\left(x + \frac{1}{2}\right)dx =1\\
&c\left[\frac{1}{2}x^2 + \frac{1}{2}x\right]_0^1 =1 \\
&c = 1
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) &= \int_0^1f(x, y)dy\\
&= \int_0^1(x+y)dy\\
&= \left[xy + \frac{1}{2}y^2\right]_0^1\\
&= x + \frac{1}{2}
\end{align*}
$$
